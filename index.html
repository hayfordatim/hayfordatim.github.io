Hayford Atim

Masters of Information Management & Systems (MIMS) 25

Hayford Atim, born in Ghana in 1993, embarked on a remarkable educational journey that has seen him complete his junior and senior high schools in Ghana before pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Actuarial Science at the University for Development Studies. Following graduation, Hayford dedicated seven years to professional consulting with Orane Consulting Limited. Driven by a thirst for academic advancement, he applied to the University of California, Berkeley, where he was admitted to the prestigious Masters of Information and Management Systems (MIMS) program. At Berkeley, Hayford is exploring a diverse range of courses, including Information Organization and Retrieval, Programming and Computation, and Qualitative Research Methods. His academic pursuit reflects a commitment to multifaceted problem-solving and the desire to contribute to the evolving field of information and management systems. Hayford Atim's journey is a testament to his unwavering dedication to education and a future filled with promise and innovation.
